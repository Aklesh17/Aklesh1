Maruti Suzuki

No real surprises on who tops the list of popular car brands in India. The Indian manufacturer offers a balanced package for middle-class families with vehicles ranging from small-city hatchbacks to premium SUVs. They provide best-in-class cabin space, mileage, service, and affordable pricing thus enabling them to dominate the Indian market. However, there are compromises on some factors such as safety and build quality on some of their entry-level products. They predominantly offer city hatchbacks and sedans with cars such as the Alto, Wagon R, Baleno, Swift, Dzire, S-Presso, Alto, Ignis, Ciaz, and much more.

Hyundai

The giant Korean-based manufacturer, having spent nearly 25 years in India has a strong foothold in the country. This has allowed it to maintain its position as number 2 in this list of popular car brands in India. Furthermore, they have now added a manufacturing facility and an R&D center in India, it is one of their most important markets globally. They are known for their reliability, attractive designs, good service network, and price range. Their portfolio today consists of affordable premium hatchbacks, sedans, and SUVs with cars such as Santro, Grand i10 Nios, i20, i20 N Line, Aura, Verna, Venue, Venue N line, Creta, in the more affordable segment and Alcazar, Tucson, and Kona Electric in the premium category which was launched recently in September 2022.

Tata

Tata is one of the popular car brands in India that has had its fair share of ups and downs. Tata Motors is a leading automotive manufacturer across passenger cars, trucks, buses, vans, and other segments. Since its acquisition of Jaguar Land Rover in 2008, its fortunes changed as its sales doubled, new factories opened and it gave jobs to thousands of employees. With the launch of its popular Nexon compact SUV, the shift in focus to better-build quality and safety helped it become a solid choice for Indian consumers and enabled it to make a solid comeback. Their product range includes quality hatchbacks, sedans, and SUVs such as the Nexon, Punch, Harrier, Safari, Altroz, Tiago, and Tigor. Tata also leads the EV segment, with the Nexon EV and Tigor EV, and the upcoming Tiago EV which is also the cheapest EV to be sold in India. It was recently unveiled by the Indian carmaker and is now expected to hit showrooms by mid-January.

Mahindra

Mahindra Auto is one of India’s most popular car brands in India building passenger and commercial vehicles along with motorcycles, tractors, buses, vans, and land defense vehicles as well. Their models are mostly known for their masculinity and ruggedness plus their renowned off-road ability. They have a wide service network across the nation and their focus on offering well-equipped SUVs at affordable prices has helped establish their status as one of India’s popular brands. Some of their popular cars include the Thar, Scorpio N, Bolero, XUV 700, and XUV 300.
