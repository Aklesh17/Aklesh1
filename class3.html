Honda

Scramblers seem to be the flavour of the season, especially in the entry-level segment.
now, even Honda are apparently formulating plans to join their rivals in this rising trend.
Based on leaked information from a website in Japan, it was revealed that Honda will be adding two scrambler motorcycles to their line-up and had already created drawings for these models back in 2018.
These drawings make it clear that the scramblers will be based on their successful cruiser siblings, the Rebel 250 and Rebel 500, respectively.
